Plot 2: My Coding Learning Journey Over Time

Question: How did my problem-solving ability and confidence improve chronologically from mid-October through early November 2025?

Why This is One of My Best Scatterplots

This visualization is one of my best because it uses Array.sort() to transform my scattered study sessions into a clear chronological narrative. By sorting the data by date from oldest (Oct 15) to newest (Nov 5), the visualization tells the complete story of my three-week coding journey—something that would be impossible to see in unsorted data.

The sort reveals a dramatic progression: I started on October 15-22 with high frustration (red circles) and low output (2-3 problems solved), struggled through a transition period in late October as I learned new concepts, then experienced a major breakthrough in early November. By November 3-5, I was consistently solving 8 problems per session with minimal frustration (green circles) and high confidence (large circles).

The dashed line connecting the points makes the trend unmistakable: my problem-solving ability improved steadily over time. But the most valuable insight comes from watching the circles change from red to green (decreasing frustration) while simultaneously growing larger (increasing confidence). This shows that my improvement wasn't just about memorizing syntax—I was genuinely becoming more comfortable with coding.

The chronological ordering was essential for answering my research question: "How did I improve over time?" Without the sort, these would just be scattered data points. With the sort, they become a clear narrative of learning, struggle, breakthrough, and mastery. The sort() method wasn't just a requirement—it was the key to transforming data into a meaningful story.
